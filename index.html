Timberborn Plant/Harvest Calculator

Notes

Work Hours:

  • Default is 16 working hours per day
  • Assumes beavers work 100% of the work day (so travel time is ignored)
  • You can enter more than 24 hours, but don't expect the results to be meaningful :)

Wellbeing:

  • Enter current average wellbeing value (global or district)
  • Negative values (from hunger/thirst) are not supported
  • Bots are not (yet) supported

Radius:

  • For a circle plot, radius signifies distance from the center, so a radius of 5 counts 5 squares in every direction (diameter of 11)
  • For a square plot, radius signifies distance from the corner, so a radius of 5 means a 5-by-5 square
  • The center/corner square is NOT counted as plantable (i.e. it's where the flag/building is)
  • For simplicity, paths and building footprints are ignored
  • Assumes perfect pathing, no food/water breaks, and 100% efficiency
  • Swimming speed is factored into aquatic crops only (not e.g. submerged forests)

Harvesters:

  • Lumberjacks for wood, harvesters for farms, gatherers for berries/nuts
  • Specify the number of beavers assigned to the plot/field, not the number of buildings

Planters:

  • Foresters for wood/berries/nuts, planters for farms
  • Specify the number of beavers assigned to the plot/field, not the number of buildings
  • Farm beavers will do both planting and harvesting (depending on priority order); adding plant/harvest times together should give a reasonable enough estimate, just keep in mind that they're probably going to be more efficient than the values shown
